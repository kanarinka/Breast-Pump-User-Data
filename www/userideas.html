What Women Want From Their Breast Pumps

by Catherine D'Ignazio

Using natural language processing, I grouped over 1000 user-submitted ideas for improving their breast pumping experience into "trigrams" - groups of three words that were often mentioned together. Here are the trigrams that were mentioned most frequently in these creative and often personal idea submissions.

1. Hands Free Bra

The most frequently cited feature that women wanted was a way to be hands-free, i.e. to not feel trapped and unable to do anything else while awkwardly pressing two cold, hard, plastic flanges against one's chest. While there are several pumps on the market that have this feature, it's important to note that only the top of the line pumps like Medela Freestyle come with this out of the box.
